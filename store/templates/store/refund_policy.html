Refund Policy

At Mens Gallery, customer satisfaction is our priority. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may request a refund under the following conditions:

  • The shoes must be unused, unworn, and in their original packaging.

  • Refunds are processed within 7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item(s).
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" are not eligible for refund or exchange.

To initiate a refund, please contact our support team at support@mensgallery.com with your order details.
